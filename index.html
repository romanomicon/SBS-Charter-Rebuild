NEHEMIAH

A Scripture Study Tool

Project
Nehemiah

A structured Bible study tool for building verse-by-verse paragraph charts across all 66 books of the canon.

Open the Library
I

Pick a Book

Choose any of the 66 canonical books of Scripture. Each book's paragraph divisions are pre-loaded and ready to work with.

II

Build the Structure

Assign paragraphs into a three-level hierarchy — Divisions, Sections, and Segments — to chart the flow of the author's argument.

III

Export the Chart

Open the Chart Editor to annotate each segment with exegetical notes, then export your completed study chart as a Word document.

Ready to begin? Your study chart awaits.
